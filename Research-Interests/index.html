我的学术研究兴趣着重于理论天体物理和理论数学领域，包括行星大气动力学、星系动力学、广义相对论、微分几何和偏微分方程。我时常惊叹于数学是宇宙万物的语言。因此，通过研究这些领域，我可以结合理论数学与天体物理来探索行星与星系的神秘。

目前正在研究类地行星磁场中的开尔文-亥姆霍兹不稳定性。包括火星。通过探索该课题，我们可以来研究行星际空间的等离子体如何进入行星磁层中,以及如何影响火星离子的逃逸。

火星空间环境,在ICB处发生开尔文-亥姆霍兹不稳定性，并引起火星离子逃逸

硕士期间研究方向为行星大气动力学。研究对象为土卫六。具体为探索土卫六高层大气的重力波耗散效应。通过建立适合于土卫六高层大气的重力波模型，可以分析重力波在土卫六高层大气是如何传播以及加热的。

My academic interests focus on the field of theoretical astrophysics and mathematics, including planetary atmospheric dynamics, galactic evolution, general relativity, differential geometry and partial differential equations. I marvel at the fact that mathematics is the language of everything in the universe. Thus, doing such researches in these fields, I can explore the mystery of the planets and galaxies combing with theoretical mathematics as well as astrophysics.

Currently, I am doing research on the Kelvin-Helmholtz instability of terrestrial planets, especially Mars. We can study how the plasmas in the interplanetary spaces into the planetary magnetosphere.

During my master study, I researched on the planetary atmospheric physics. The research object is Titan, which is the largest moon of Saturn. Under the guidance of my master advisor, I had analyzed the gravity wave propagation in Titan’s upper atmosphere through establishing an anelastic linearized wave model.
