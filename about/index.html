欢迎访问我的个人网站！ 我叫StarKing, 现在是澳门科技大学的地球与行星科学博士。目前我是太空科学研究所月球与行星科学国家重点实验室太空物理团组的一名成员。

我于2020年毕业于中国科学院大学。在硕士期间，我曾是中国科学院国家天文台月球与深空探测研究部行星物理团组以及中山大学行星环境与宜居性研究实验室的成员, 同时在崔峻教授和连源博士的指导下，对土星最大的卫星土卫六的高层大气重力波展开详细的研究。

在进入中国科学院大学之前，我获得了华中农业大学的数学理学学士学位。本科期间，我主修数学的同时，同时辅修武汉大学的经济学双学位。

我的故乡位于湖南省衡阳市衡南县。“衡南”是指衡山的南部。衡山是中国五岳之一，称为南岳。

在2008年，我进入衡南县第二中学就读高中。在高一时深深地被高中物理课本中“机遇号”火星车所吸引。因此对天文学开始有着浓厚的兴趣，特别是行星科学探测。此外，我也对数学和物理非常感兴趣。

在日常生活中，我喜欢旅游、摄影、音乐、电影。

Welcome to my personal website! My name is StarKing. I am a Ph.D student in Earth and planetary sciences at Macau University of Science and Technology(M.S.U.T). Currently I am a member of Space Physics Group in State Key Laboratory of Lunar and Planetary Sciences of Space Sciences Institute.

In 2020, I graduated from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences(UCAS). When I was a master student, I was a member of Planetary Physics Group in National Astronomical Observatories, Chinese Academy of Sciences(NAOC) and Planetary Environmental and Astrobiological Research Laboratory (PEARL) at Sun Yat-Sen University. Besides, I worked with Prof. Jun Cui on planetary atmospheres as well as cooperate with Dr.Yuan Lian on the upper atmosphere gravity waves in the Saturn’s largest moon: Titan.

Before I was admitted to UCAS, I received my B.S. in Mathematics from Huazhong Agricultural University(HZAU). I major in mathematics at HZAU and minor in economics at Wuhan University (WHU) when I was an undergraduate student.

My hometown is located in Hengnan county, HengYang, Hunan Province, China. “Hengnan” means it is located in the south of Mount Heng, where is one of China’s famous mountains.

In 2008, I went to Hengnan No.2 high school. I am interested in astronomy since when I see a picture of Mars Exploration Rover–B in my physics textbook. Besides, I am also interested in mathematic and physics. So I chose Astrophysics as my major in gradate level without hesitation.

In my daily life, I like travel, movies(especially science fiction movies), music,photography.
